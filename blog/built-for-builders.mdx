Developers don't want hand-holding. They want **control, automation, and zero lock-in**. Stackryze Domains was designed with that in mind from day one.

## Manage your way

Whether you prefer clicking through a dashboard or scripting everything from your terminal, we've got you covered.

- **Intuitive dashboard** — Manage records visually with a clean, fast UI
- **API-driven workflows** — Automate domain and DNS operations end-to-end
- **Bring your own nameservers** — Use Cloudflare, Route 53, or anything else
- **Hosting integrations** — Point at Vercel, Netlify, Fly, your VPS — your call

## Complete DNS control

Every record type you'd expect, with none of the restrictions:

| Record | Use case |
| --- | --- |
| **A / AAAA** | Point to an IP address |
| **CNAME** | Alias to another domain |
| **TXT** | Verification, SPF, DKIM |
| **MX** | Custom email routing |
| **NS** | Delegate to external nameservers |
