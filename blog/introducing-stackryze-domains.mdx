## Why we exist

We're a small team of students with a passion for making the internet more open and accessible. Stackryze Domains is our contribution to the chaotic, beautiful mess that is the open web.

Claim your domain. Point it anywhere. Deploy. No strings attached.

## What you get

- **Forever Free** — No hidden fees, no subscriptions, no upsells
- **Full DNS Control** — A, CNAME, TXT, and more
- **Instant Setup** — Sign in with GitHub and you're live in seconds
- **No Vendor Lock-in** — Use our DNS or bring your own (Cloudflare, etc.)
- **Reliable Infrastructure** — Globally distributed, continuously monitored
